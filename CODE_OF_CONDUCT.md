# Code of conduct

## Our commitment

Open Gov Flows is a community project serving public sector organisations across the UK. We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, respectful environment for everyone who contributes — regardless of experience level, background, or organisation type.

We expect all participants to engage constructively and in good faith.

---

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Give and receive feedback constructively
- Focus criticism on work, not people
- Acknowledge different perspectives and experience levels
- Help others learn — not everyone is a developer
- Respect that contributors participate in their own time

---

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Dismissive or demeaning language toward contributors
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional public sector setting

---

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests and any other forum where contributors interact on behalf of this project.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who do not meet the standards of this community.
